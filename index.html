Renewable Energy

┌──────────────────── •✧• ────────────────────┐

What is it?

Use of natural energy from the planet (Sun, Geothermal, Wind, etc.) to create cars and other devices without causing Carbon Dioxide to go into the planet's atmosphere.

Benefits to society

Creates job creation throughout renewable energy industries. It also allows for abundant, affordable, secure, safe, and clean energy, which is beneficial to humans.

Consequences to society

It may generate toxic substances that may contaminate water resources. Renewable energy installations can also disrupt ecosystems and land use and may use significant amounts of water.

Components of technology required for this Trend

Batteries and photovoltaic (PV) arrays

Careers related to this Trend

Greenhouse Worker, Recycling Worker, Environmental Technician, Urban Planner, Solar Installer, etc.

Website of value to learn more about the Trend

Department of Energy (.gov), https://www.energy.gov/eere/renewable-energy

Quantum Computing.

┌──────────────────── •✧• ────────────────────┐

What is it?

Quantum computing, unlike classical computing, is built on Qubits rather than bits.

Benefits to society

Quantum Computers can solve problems significantly faster than classical computers. In 2019, Google announced that a quantum computer solved a problem in 200 seconds (about 3 and a half minutes) that would take a normal computer 10, 000 years. The vast number of solutions it can come up with can make it easier to develop models of molecules in the human body, accelerate the training of AI system, and do prime factorization. This can help the pharmaceutical companies develop drugs, help the automotive companies teach self-driving cars how to make decisions in crucial moments, and factorize large prime numbers that businesses as the basis for their encryption efforts.

Consequences to society

One of the main concerns with quantum computing is cybersecurity, especially in the event of large-scale quantum adoption. This means in the geopolitical space whoever gets their first has a huge advantage.

Components of technology required for this Trend

Some quantum computers implement qubits using electronic states of charged atoms called ions. Trapped ion-based systems apply quantum gates using lasers to manipulate the electronic state of the ion.

Careers related to this Trend

Quantum Network Security Engineer, Quantum Systems Technician, Quantum Mechanics Developer, Quantum Hardware/Software Engineer

Website of value to learn more about the Trend

https://www.ibm.com/topics/quantum-computing

Electrical Vehicles

┌──────────────────── •✧• ────────────────────┐

What is it?

Electric Vehicle also known as EV is a vehicle that can be powered by an electric motor which brings electricity from a battery and is capable of being charged from an external source.

Benefits to society

Using EV’s addresses an important public health hazard. They can also lower vehicle fueling costs by half, or more.

Consequences to society

EV battery production can adversely impact the environment, such as a loss of biodiversity, air pollution and decreased freshwater supply.

Components of technology required for this Trend

Head unit, ECU, Electric Drivetrain, Electric Transmission, Motors, Lithium Battery

Careers related to this Trend

Electrical Engineer, Lithium Miner, Iron Miner, Welder, Software Engineer, Factory/Assembly line Manager Car Dealership Manager, Salesman, International affairs/sales manager, Copper Miner

Website of value to learn more about the Trend

https://ww2.arb.ca.gov/, This is an important website because it is the California government site so its reliable information and California was the first state to implement mandatory electric vehicles laws.

Cloud

┌──────────────────── •✧• ────────────────────┐

What is it?

Simply put, Cloud computing is storing and accessing data and computing services over the internet rather than using local hard drives and personal data centers.

Benefits to society

The benefits to society from using Cloud Computing is getting their applications to market quickly.

Consequences to society

There are, or rather, lack thereof secure API’s. It is easy to access without authentication, which leads to vulnerability.

Components of technology required for this Trend

The technology required for Cloud Computing is Client Infrastructure, Application, Service, Runtime Cloud, Storage, Infrastructure, Management, Security, and Internet.

Careers related to this Trend

Some careers related to cloud computing are cloud administrator, cloud support engineer, cloud security analyst, cloud network engineer, cloud software engineer, and cloud automation engineer

VR AR

┌──────────────────── •✧• ────────────────────┐

What is it?

AR- Augmented reality is an enhanced reality that uses text, graphics, and audio along with other enhanced integrated real- time objects. VR- Virtual reality is the use of computer modeling and simulation that allows a person to interact with an artifact that is three dimensional in a sensory environment.

Benefits to society

Allows Real-Time applications, Enhances Interactive learning, Increases of users' engagement, allows Revolutionary Healthcare, boosts Creativity, helps understand complex concepts

Consequences to society

Concerns with VR are the headaches, nausea, and eyestrains caused when using for too long, concerns with AR are the possibilities of physical harm, security and privacy issues, social issues.

Components of technology required for this Trend

For AR you need a processor, input devices, sensors, & a display. For VR you need software, headset, audio system, input devices, sensors, processor.

Careers related to this Trend

Virtual Reality Engineer, Visual Systems Designer, AR/VR Camera Driver Engineer, AR/VR System Software Engineer, Performance Engineer

Website of value to learn more about the Trend

https://edu.gcfglobal.org/en/thenow/understanding-virtual-reality-and-augmented-reality/1/
